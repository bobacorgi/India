The National Flag of India symbolizes India's pride and represents the aspirations of all Indians. The National Flag was adopted on July 22, 1947, and symbolized Democracy in India.

The current president of india is Droupadi Murmu. Droupadi Murmu is an Indian politician who is serving as the 15th and current President of India since 25 July 2022.
